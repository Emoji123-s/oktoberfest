• Strawberry Cheesecake Burritos

    Ingredients

    • Strawberries - 1/2 Cup
    • Vanilla - 1 tablespoon
    • Sour Cheese - 3/4 cup
    • Sugar - 1/2 cup
    • Vegetable Oil - 1/2 cup
    • Tortillas - 2
    • Toothpicks

    Method of Preparation

    Add cream cheese, sour cheese, sugar and vanilla extract and mix thoroughly. Add in the strawberries and mix well

    Warm the tortillas. Place the filling at the end side and roll together to form a small wrap. Hold with a toothpick

    Heat oil in a pan. Fry until golden. Take out of the oil and remove toothpick. Serve with strawberry slices.
