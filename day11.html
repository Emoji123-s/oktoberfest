• Carrot Rice

    Ingredients

    • Roasted Peanuts - 1/4 cup
    • Margarine - 1 tablespoon
    • Onion - 1, Thinly sliced
    • Fresh Ginger - 1 tablespoon
    • Grated Carrots - 3/4 cup
    • Salt and Pepper
    • Rice - 1 cup, cooked

    Method of Preparation

    Blend the peanuts and set aside. melt the margarine and add the onions. Cook for 5 minutes.

    Add in the ginger and fry for 1-2 minutes. Add the carrots and fry for 2-3 minutes. Add salt to taste and stir.

    Reduce the heat and cover for 5 minutes. Add pepper to taste and the blended peanuts. Mix well.

    Add cooked rice and stir thoroughly till the mixture is even. Garnish with lettuce or spinach.
