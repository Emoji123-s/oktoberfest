• Brookie's Pancakes

    Ingredients

    For the Pancakes

    • Flour - 1 cup
    • White Sugar - 2 tablespoons
    • Baking Powder - 1 teaspoon
    • Baking Soda - 1/4 teaspoon
    • Salt - 1/4 teaspoon
    • Buttermilk - 1 cup
    • Egg - 1
    • Melted Butter - 2 tablespoons
    • Vanilla - 1 teaspoon
    • Chocolate Chips - ½ cup

    For the Chocolate Swirl

    • Chocolate powder - 1 box
    • Water - 1 cup
    • Oil - 1 cup
    • Egg - 2

    Method of Preparation

    Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a bowl. In another bowl, mix buttermilk, an egg, butter and vanilla. Pour into the dry ingredient and mix. Add in chocolate chips and mix.

    In a bowl, mix chocloate powder, water, oil and eggs. Pour in a bag and close tightly.

    Heat up a frying pan with a teaspoon of oil or non-stick spray. Pour in one cooking spoon of the batter. Punch a hole holding the chocolate batter and pump 5 circles in the batter. Swirl with a toothpick or a kebab stick.

    Fry 3 minutes on both sides. Serve with more chocolate drizzle.
