• Banana Muffins

    Ingredients

    • Flour - 3 cups
    • Brown Sugar - ½ cup
    • White Sugar - 1 cup
    • Cinnamon - 2 tablespoons
    • Baking Powder - 2 tablespoons
    • Baking Soda - 1 teaspoon
    • Nutmeg - 1 teaspoon
    • Salt - 1 teaspoon
    • Ripe Bananas - 2, mashed
    • Canola Oil - 1 cup
    • Coconut Milk - 1 cup

    Method of Preparation

    Mix flour, brown sugar, white sugar, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, salt and nutmeg in a bowl.

    In another bowl, mix mashed bananas, canola oil and coconut milk. Pour into the dry ingredient and mix together.

    Put muffin cups in a muffin pan and add 1 scoop of the batter into each cup. Bake at 350°F for 30-35 minutes.
