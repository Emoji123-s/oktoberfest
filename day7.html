• Melting Moments

    Ingredients

    • Flour - 1 cup
    • Flavoring (Preferably Vanilla) - 1 tablespoon
    • Corn Starch - 1/4 cup
    • Powdered Sugar - ½ cup
    • Unsalted Butter - 1 cup

    Method of Preparation

    Whip butter and sugar for 4-5 minutes. Add vaniila extract and mix. Add corn starch and half a cup of flour. Mix well. Add the rest of the flour and mix until smooth

    Use a piping bag with any tip you like to make cookies, or using an ice-cream scoop, scoop out one spoon of the batter onto a sheet tray

    Bake 20 minutes at 180°C

    Serve with Milk
