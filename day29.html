• Peas and Sausage Pasta

    Ingredients

    • Oil - 2 tablespoons
    • Garlic - 1 clove
    • Turkey Sausage - 1 pound, ground
    • Frozen Peas - 1 pack
    • Thick Cream - 1½ cup
    • Butter - 4 tablespoons
    • Pasta - 1 pack

    Method of Preparation

    In a pan. add in the oil and garlic. Saute a little, and add the turkey sausage and frozen peas. Simmer for 5 minutes.

    Add thick cream and butter. Bring to the boil and cook for 5 minutes. Add the cooked pasta and mix well.
