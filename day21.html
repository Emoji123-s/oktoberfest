• ButterMilk Pie

    Ingredients

    • Eggs - 3
    • Vanilla - 1 tablespoon
    • Butter - 1 stick
    • Sugar - 1 cup
    • Buttermilk - 1 cup
    • Flour - 1/4 cup
    • Lemon Juice - 2 tablespoons
    • Nutmeg - ½ teaspoon
    • Pie Crust (Unbaked)

    Method of Preparation

    Beat 3 eggs till frothy. Add butter, sugar and flour and whisk. Stir in buttermilk, vanilla, lemon juice and nutmeg

    Pour into a pie dish lined with pie crust and bake until center is firm, at least 40-60 minutes.
