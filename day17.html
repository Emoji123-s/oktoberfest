• Microwave Chocolate Mug Cake

    Ingredients

    • Mug - 1
    • Vanilla - 1/4 teaspoon
    • Flour - 1/4 cup
    • Sugar - 1/4 cup
    • Cocoa Powder - 2 tablespoons
    • Baking Soda - 1/8 teaspoon
    • Milk - 2 tablespoons
    • Oil - 2 tablespoons
    • Water - 1 tablespoon

    Method of Preparation

    In a mug, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda and salt. Add milk, oil, water, and vanilla and mix well.

    Microwave for 1 minute and 45 seconds
