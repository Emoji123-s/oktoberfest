• Choco-Truffle Cookies

    Ingredients

    • Unsweetened Chocolate - 4 squares
    • Semi-sweet Chocolate Chips - 1 cup
    • Butter - 6 tablespoons
    • Eggs - 3
    • Sprinkles
    • Sugar - 1 cup
    • Vanilla - 1½ teaspoons
    • Flour - ½ cup
    • Unsweetened Cocoa Powder - 2 tablespoons
    • Baking Powder - 1/4 teaspoon
    • Salt - 1/4 teaspoon
    • Semi-sweet Chocolate Chips - 1 cup

    Method of Preparation

    Break chocolate squares, pour into a bowl and add butter and chocolate chips and melt, then cool.

    In another bowl, add eggs and butter and whip till pale. Add vanilla and the chocolate mixture and mix till combined.

    In another bowl, add flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt. Mix and pour into the chocolate mixture. Add chocolate chips and mix. Cover and chill for one hour

    Roll the chilled dough into balls and place them in any sprinkles of your choice. Place on a tray and bake for 9-11 minutes at 350°F. Cool completely and serve.
