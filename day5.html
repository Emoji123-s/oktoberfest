• Fried Muffins

    Ingredients

    • Flour - ½ cup
    • Salt - a pinck
    • Sugar - 2 teaspoons
    • Milk - 1/4 Cup

    Method of Preparation

    Knead all the ingredients together to make a firm dough. Flatten with a rolling pin to about 6mm thick, then cut into 10cm rounds

    Place a cast-iron pan on medium heat and sprinkle with flour. Place the muffin circles in the pan and brown on both sides for about 10 minutes a side

    They will puff-up about 4 times their original height, as they are fairly plain

    Serve
