• Choco-Mallow Bars

    Ingredients

    • Chocolate brownie mix - 1 pack
    • Oil - 1 cup
    • Water - ½ cup
    • Egg - 2
    • Mini Marshmellow - 1 pack
    • Chocolate Chips - 1½ cups
    • Peanut Butter (Creamy) - 1 cup
    • Butter - 1 cup
    • Rice Cereal - 1 cup

    Method of Preparation

    Mix chocolate brownie mix, oil, water and eggs together. Pour into a greased baking dish. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean, 28-35 minutes.

    Top with marshmellow and bake until slightly melted, 3 minutes. Remove anc cool completely.

    Melt chocolate chips, peanut butter and butter. Stir in rice cereal off heat. Pour on cooled brownie. Chill until firm.
